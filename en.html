Why Me?

That is a great question. Through the experience I have gained so far, I have met different clients and different requirements. In most cases, the requirements were quite challenging, which contributed to my development, both in design and in relation to clients. A good client-designer relationship is extremely important when creating the ideal solution. Clients recognize this, so there are also recommendations that expand my scope of work, which is confirmed by my portfolio.

On the other hand, in every business, including this one, it is important to develop and not stand still. Otherwise, trends and various innovations will go far ahead of you and that is where the quality of work gets lost. In our business, quality is crucial. A large number of designers, both in our and foreign markets, are just running behind the trends, which is very evident when creating a targeted solution for the client. This is the problem of today, in which the market is oversaturated with designers who arbitrarily call themselves designers. There is graphic design all around us and this can be easily noticed and assessed. Unfortunately, many will present themselves to you in their best light, but as in life and o in graphic design, works are a much better representation of the designer.

My works will surely be a great presentation of me, my skills and everything I told you about.

My vision

In creating a vision, it is also very important to develop, follow trends and upgrade your skills and knowledge. It simply accompanies us in every sphere of graphic design. In graphic design, it is a big problem to stay popular on the market without it. Therefore, the vision changed as did the personal upgrading of knowledge and experience. When I entered the world of design, I had no idea that one day I would be where I am now. Obviously effort, work and discipline all come around, sooner or later.

After gaining some knowledge and experience, one lesson came up, which unfortunately most people never learn or understand. This work is done in order to "transfer" the necessary service, that is, the idea, to the client on paper, and to implement it in the target industry, and then we get to the payment, after we have satisfied the client. Unfortunately, many do the exact opposite and in a totally wrong way. Their idea is to get the money, and that is why they have chosen to do this job.

Providing the client with a solution to their idea, or offering ideas to a client without any ideas and creating an appropriate solution that will meet the client's expectations with extremely friendly communication that does not really exist nowadays, is the primary thing. This way, I expect clients to recognize quality work, good relationships and communication. In time, former and future clients will be acquainted with the fact that all graphic services can be done at one place and that collaboration is possible in the long run!
